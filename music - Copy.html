5-Week Plan for Learning Singing

Week 1: Foundation and Breathing Techniques

Develop basic vocal technique and control your breath.

  • Focus on diaphragmatic breathing
  • Practice posture for better airflow
  • Start with simple vocal warm-ups
  • Practice pitch control with a piano or tuning app
  • Choose a simple song to sing and practice slowly

Week 2: Expanding Vocal Range and Tone

Begin expanding your vocal range and improving tone quality.

  • Practice scale exercises for low and high notes
  • Focus on singing in tune with a piano or tuning app
  • Work on maintaining a clear, smooth tone
  • Strengthen your voice by sustaining notes

Week 3: Improving Technique and Vocal Power

Improve vocal control, power, and projection.

  • Work on projecting your voice with good resonance
  • Practice singing with variations in volume
  • Develop power in your singing without straining
  • Start adding a natural vibrato to your voice

Week 4: Song Repertoire and Performance Skills

Focus on singing full songs with emotion and confidence.

  • Learn new songs that challenge your range and skills
  • Work on memorizing lyrics and phrasing
  • Develop performance skills like facial expressions and stage presence
  • Practice putting emotion into your singing

Week 5: Polishing and Preparing for a Performance

Polish your singing and prepare for a full performance.

  • Perfect your songs and smooth out rough parts
  • Ensure proper breathing control
  • Refine your tone, pitch, and range
  • Practice performing in front of a mirror or record yourself
